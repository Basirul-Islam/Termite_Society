WELLCOME TO TERMITE

The Termite Society, a science-based non-political, non-profit, research-based environmental organization established with some enthusiastic students of creative and inquisitive minds who spontaneously express their views and innovative ideas & works for the execution on fixing the identified problems by acquiring scientific methods. The society usually publishes environmental magazine named “The Termite” besides arranges different workshops, seminars, awareness raising activities, training sessions, tree plantation programs with the involvement of people in Bangladesh as well as beyond the country.

Histroy

The journey was started informally on 6th December, 2016 at University of Dhaka and on 24th February, 2017 with an official announcement through the relentless hard work of environment conscious students of University of Barishal, Bangladesh. Later the society reached different universities across the country & the expand works in those campuses. The first phase of the activities of the Termite Society revealed on 5th June, 2017 with the publication of the first issue of 'The Termite' named 'Bilupta Pray' (বিলুপ্ত প্রায়) & moving forward with success.

Aims & Objectives

Observing the ongoing global environmental catastrophe, the greenhouse response, man-made climate disasters, the Termite Society has begun its journey with a view to work in accordance with the goals of Sustainable Development Goals (SDG), take action to reduce & prevent pollution, arrange training session in developing the skills in different environmental aspects & work for green environment, raise awareness in all sectors especially children to be aware to build a sustainable environment for future
